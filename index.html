Dashboard Guide

Target Decay System: Your daily target % stays fixed for Days 1–50. From Day 51, it decreases gradually as your capital grows, reducing liquidation risk. Days 91–100 use a very tight target (~0.5–1%) to lock in compounded gains.

Worst-Case Loss (15%): If you lose more than 15% of your current balance in a single day, that's outside the risk envelope. Use this as your hard stop-loss reference.

Performance Engine: Scored across 4 axes — Win Rate (30%), Consistency via EMA (30%), Discipline via trade quality (25%), and Risk Management (15%). XP compounds with streaks.

Edit & Delete: Any logged day can be edited or deleted. All subsequent days cascade-recalculate automatically to keep your balance chain accurate.
